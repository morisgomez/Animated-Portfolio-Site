Hi!

My name is Moris, and I immigrated from El Salvador to Los Angeles when I was three. I'm currently a third year DACA recipient at UCLA. I'm also the oldest of three siblings. In 2018, my father was incarcerated, so me and my mother opened up a beauty salon in South LA as a source of income. I began developing a passion for design and programming after I created a website for the salon. Now, I'm slowly transitioning into front-end development and design.
